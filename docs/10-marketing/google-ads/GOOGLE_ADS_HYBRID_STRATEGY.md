# üéØ WHY THIS STRATEGY WORKS

## ‚úÖ Advantages:

1. **Risk Mitigation**
   - Only risk aggressive bidding on proven campaigns
   - Keep learners safe and controlled

2. **Fast Results on Winners**
   - VIC + EduTest (70% of revenue) get optimized immediately
   - Don't waste time on slow learning

3. **Safe Learning on Others**
   - ACER, NAPLAN campaigns collect data safely
   - Can graduate when ready

4. **Budget Efficiency**
   - 70% of budget ($70/day) on proven winners
   - 30% of budget ($40/day) on learners
   - Optimal allocation

5. **Flexibility**
   - Can promote learners to conversions anytime
   - Can demote back to clicks if issues
   - Not locked into one strategy
