# üìù NOTES & TIPS

## **Why Exact + Phrase Only?**
- ‚úÖ Maximum control over search queries
- ‚úÖ Minimal wasted spend
- ‚úÖ High-quality, high-intent traffic only
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Lower volume than broad match
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Slower data collection (2-3 months vs 4-6 weeks)

## **When to Add Broad Match:**
If after 30 days you're getting:
- Less than 50 clicks/week total
- Less than 2 conversions/week
- Less than 200 impressions/day

Then consider adding 3-5 broad match keywords per campaign.

## **Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
- ‚ùå Don't change bidding strategy during first 2 weeks
- ‚ùå Don't pause keywords with low impressions (they might convert!)
- ‚ùå Don't forget to add new negative keywords weekly
- ‚ùå Don't make multiple changes at once

## **Success Indicators (Week 2):**
- ‚úÖ 300+ impressions/day across all campaigns
- ‚úÖ 15+ clicks/day across all campaigns
- ‚úÖ 1-2 conversions/week minimum
- ‚úÖ Search terms are all relevant (no "laptop" or "free" searches)

---

# üÜò TROUBLESHOOTING

## **If Impressions Are Too Low (<100/day):**
1. Increase max CPC to $3.50
2. Add 5-10 more keyword variations
3. Check if campaigns are "Limited by budget"
4. Consider adding broad match for top performers

## **If CTR Is Low (<3%):**
1. Improve ad copy (add benefits, urgency)
2. Make sure ads link to specific product pages
3. Add negative keywords to filter out bad searches
4. Pause low-performing keywords

## **If No Conversions After 2 Weeks:**
1. Check if conversion tracking is working
2. Verify ads link to correct product pages (not homepage!)
3. Review landing page (is it compelling?)
4. Check if price ($199) is clear and visible
5. Consider reducing friction in checkout process
