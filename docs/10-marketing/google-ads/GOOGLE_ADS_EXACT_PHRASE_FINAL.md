# üí° PRO TIPS

## 1. Why Exact + Phrase Together?

**Exact match [keyword]:**
- Catches people searching EXACTLY for that term
- Highest intent
- Lowest volume
- Lowest CPC

**Phrase match "keyword":**
- Catches variations with the phrase in it
- High intent
- Medium volume
- Medium CPC

**Together = Best of both worlds!**

---

## 2. Why These Negatives?

**Excluded from negatives:**
- ‚ùå "free" - People searching "free practice test" might buy after trying
- ‚ùå "sample" - Want to see samples before buying
- ‚ùå "pdf" - Looking for downloadable materials (we have this!)
- ‚ùå "papers" - "practice papers" shows high purchase intent
- ‚ùå "download" - Want to download practice materials

**Included in negatives:**
- ‚úÖ Wrong products (other tests, ACER laptops)
- ‚úÖ Wrong audiences (teachers, tutors, jobs)
- ‚úÖ Wrong intent (login, results, marking guides)
- ‚úÖ Commercial blockers (cheap, refurbished, used - for ACER)

# üö® TROUBLESHOOTING

## Issue 1: Low Impressions (<100/day total)

**Causes:**
- Keywords too specific
- Max CPC too low
- Budget too low

**Solutions:**
1. Increase max CPC to $3.50
2. Add 20-30 more keyword variations
3. Increase daily budgets by 50%
4. Check if campaigns are "Limited by budget"

---

## Issue 2: High Impressions but Low Clicks (<2% CTR)

**Causes:**
- Ads not compelling
- Keywords don't match ad copy
- Landing page not relevant in ad preview

**Solutions:**
1. Improve ad headlines (add benefits, urgency)
2. Ensure ad copy includes keyword text
3. Verify ad URLs point to correct pages
4. Add more specific keywords (less broad)

---

## Issue 3: Good Clicks but No Conversions

**Causes:**
- Conversion tracking not working
- Landing page not converting
- Price objection ($199)
- Wrong audience

**Solutions:**
1. Test conversion tracking (make test purchase)
2. Review landing page conversion rate
3. Check search terms for irrelevant queries
4. Add more negative keywords
5. Consider A/B testing price

---

## Issue 4: ACER Getting Computer Searches

**Symptoms:**
- Search terms show "acer laptop", "acer chromebook"
- High clicks, 0 conversions
- Wasted spend

**Solutions:**
1. Verify all 141 ACER negatives added
2. Add more computer-related negatives
3. Check if negatives set to "Phrase match" (not broad)
4. Consider pausing broad match ACER keywords

---

## Issue 5: Campaign Paused by Google

**Causes:**
- Low quality score
- Very low CTR (<0.5%)
- Policy violation

**Solutions:**
1. Check email from Google for reason
2. Improve ad quality
3. Increase relevance (keyword ‚Üí ad ‚Üí landing page)
4. Appeal if policy violation incorrect
