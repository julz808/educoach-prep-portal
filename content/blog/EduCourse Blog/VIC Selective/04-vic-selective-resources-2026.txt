Best VIC Selective Entry Resources 2026: Complete Guide

INTRODUCTION:

Preparing for Victoria's Selective Entry test requires high-quality resources that accurately reflect the actual test format, difficulty, and question styles. With five demanding sections—General Ability Verbal (60 Q), General Ability Quantitative (50 Q), Mathematics Reasoning (60 Q), Reading Reasoning (50 Q), and Writing (40 min)—students need comprehensive materials covering all areas.

The challenge? The market offers dozens of preparation books, online platforms, tutoring services, and practice tests with varying quality, accuracy, and value. Some resources closely mirror the actual test while others use outdated formats or inappropriate difficulty levels.

This comprehensive guide reviews the best VIC Selective Entry preparation resources for 2026, including official materials, top-rated books, online platforms, and tutoring options. We provide honest assessments of pros, cons, pricing, and which resources work best for different preparation needs.


H2: Official VCAA Materials

The gold standard for preparation comes directly from the test creator.

H3: Official Practice Tests

**What They Are:**
The Victorian Curriculum and Assessment Authority (VCAA) releases official practice materials including sample questions and practice tests that mirror the actual Selective Entry test format and difficulty.

**Pros:**
✓ Most accurate representation of actual test format
✓ Question styles match real test exactly
✓ Difficulty calibration is authentic
✓ Free or low-cost
✓ Essential for every student

**Cons:**
✗ Limited quantity (typically 1-2 practice tests available)
✗ No detailed answer explanations
✗ No adaptive practice by sub-skill
✗ Insufficient volume for complete preparation

**Pricing:**
Free (available on VCAA website)

**Best For:**
Every student preparing for VIC Selective. Use these as benchmark assessments and familiarization tools.

**How to Use:**
- Start with sample questions to understand format
- Save full practice tests for Weeks 10 and 13 of preparation
- Treat as dress rehearsals under exact test conditions
- Supplement with other resources for comprehensive practice

**Verdict:**
★★★★★ (5/5) - Essential but insufficient alone

**Avoid Resources That:**

✗ Don't specify VIC Selective (generic "selective schools")

**Look For Resources That:**
✓ Clearly state VIC Selective focus

✓ Provide sample content for evaluation
